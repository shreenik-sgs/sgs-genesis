# PART I — FOUNDATION

## EIOS-001 Mission

The Economic Intelligence Operating System (EIOS) exists to observe reality, discover reusable economic knowledge,
maintain a continuously evolving world model, and convert validated scientific understanding into explainable,
evidence-backed investment intelligence.

## EIOS-002 Design Principles

The following principles are permanent architectural invariants.

1. Reality before models.
2. Systems before companies.
3. Causality before correlation.
4. Scientific reasoning before prediction.
5. Knowledge before recommendations.
6. Explainability by default.
7. Continuous learning.
8. Institutional memory.

## EIOS-003 Constitution

Every subsystem SHALL comply with the Unified Specification.
Genesis SHALL create knowledge.
Prometheus SHALL consume published knowledge.
All production recommendations SHALL be reproducible through historical replay.
Architectural changes require an Architecture Decision Record (ADR).

## EIOS-004 Core Definitions

Reality Observation: A verified observation of the external world.
Evidence: Corroborated observations.
Signal: A statistically or scientifically meaningful pattern.
Theme: Multiple related signals.
Megatrend: Multiple reinforcing themes.
Economic Law: A validated, replay-tested causal principle.

## EIOS-005 Platform Scope

Included:
• Scientific discovery
• World modelling
• Economic reasoning
• Investment intelligence
• Portfolio decision support

Excluded:
• High-frequency trading
• Black-box prediction
• Opaque recommendations
• Human decision replacement
