# Volume 0 - Part XIII: Systems Science & Complex Adaptive Systems

## Canonical System Layers
- **Physical**: Energy, minerals, infrastructure, climate, geography
- **Biological**: Population, health, agriculture
- **Technological**: Semiconductors, AI, robotics, telecom
- **Industrial**: Manufacturing, logistics, supply chains
- **Economic**: Demand, supply, pricing, capital formation
- **Financial**: Markets, liquidity, valuation, credit
- **Behavioral**: Consumers, institutions, policy makers
- **Portfolio**: Capital allocation and investment decisions

## Constitutional Rules
- Every graph MUST map to one or more real systems.
- Every recommendation MUST identify affected system layers.
- Economic Laws SHOULD be expressed as system behaviors, not company behaviors.
- Digital Twins MUST preserve cross-layer dependencies.
- System interactions MUST remain explainable and replayable.
