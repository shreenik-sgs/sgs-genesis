# Volume 0 - Part VIII: Hypothesis Lifecycle & Scientific Evolution

## Lifecycle States
- **Observed**: Initial observation recorded.
- **Emerging**: Preliminary hypothesis with limited evidence.
- **Competing**: Multiple explanations coexist.
- **Validated**: Evidence and replay support the hypothesis.
- **Provisionally Accepted**: Operationally usable while monitoring.
- **Economic Principle**: Generalized recurring relationship.
- **Economic Law**: Broadly validated and reusable.
- **Superseded**: Replaced by stronger explanation.
- **Falsified**: Evidence contradicts the hypothesis.

## Constitutional Rules
- Every hypothesis retains complete lineage.
- Falsified knowledge is archived, not deleted.
- Economic Laws remain challengeable.
- Promotion requires reproducible evidence.
- Operational success alone is insufficient for scientific promotion.
