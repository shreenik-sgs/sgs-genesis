# GPSR 000 Platform Constitution

- Reality is the starting point; stock prices are the final output.
- Historical research (Genesis) and live decision making (Prometheus) are separated.
- Persistent World Graphs represent enduring structures of the economy.
- Activation Contexts determine which graph regions matter.
- The Capital Discovery Pipeline is the primary product.
- Every recommendation must be explainable.
- No weighted composite scores.
- Every hypothesis includes evidence, counter-evidence, unknowns and confidence.
- Recursive Self-Improvement updates Genesis only after validation.
- Every subsystem must improve at least one pipeline stage.
