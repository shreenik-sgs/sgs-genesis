# Volume 0 - Part I: Vision, Philosophy & First Principles

## First Principles
1. Reality changes before markets react.
1. Multiple signals create a Theme.
1. Multiple Themes create a Megatrend.
1. Megatrends reshape Economic Graphs.
1. Economic Graphs determine future profit pools.
1. Profit pools determine future corporate winners.
1. Technical analysis optimizes execution, not idea generation.
1. Knowledge compounds through recursive scientific learning.

## Non-Negotiable Design Rules
- Everything is graph-first.
- Everything has provenance.
- Everything is versioned.
- Everything is replayable.
- No permanent knowledge without validation.
- No recommendation without an explainable reasoning chain.
- Scientific truth overrides model confidence.
- Architecture favors long-term correctness over short-term convenience.
