# GPSR-019 Leadership Intelligence Engine

Purpose: Evaluate leadership as long-term capital compounders.

## Evaluation Dimensions
- Capital allocation discipline
- Execution against strategy
- Innovation and R&D effectiveness
- Product vision
- Talent attraction and retention
- Operational excellence
- Governance quality
- Incentive alignment
- M&A quality
- Communication credibility
- Adaptability through market cycles
- Founder advantage (where applicable)

## Processing Pipeline
1. Load candidate companies
2. Assemble leadership evidence
3. Analyze historical decisions
4. Compare against successful leadership archetypes
5. Identify strengths and risks
6. Generate Leadership Assessment
7. Forward to Technical Timing Engine
