# Volume 0 - Part VI: Time, Causality & Economic Dynamics

## Five Time Horizons
- Historical (Genesis): What happened? -> Historical World Graphs
- Present (Prometheus): What is happening? -> Economic Digital Twin
- Projected (Prometheus): What may happen? -> Scenario Graphs
- Decision (Personal CIO): What should we do? -> Portfolio Actions
- Learning (Recursive Improvement): What did we learn? -> Knowledge Promotions

## Constitutional Rules
- Time is part of every canonical object.
- Causal links take precedence over temporal coincidence.
- Scenario graphs never overwrite observed history.
- Historical replay must reconstruct prior world states exactly.
- Every prediction expires or is resolved into observed outcomes.
