# GPSR-022 Exit & Capital Rotation Engine

Purpose: Optimize long-term compounding by determining when to hold, trim, exit, and rotate capital.

## Exit Triggers
- Original investment thesis invalidated
- Value capture migrating elsewhere
- Capital cycle reaching maturity
- Superior asymmetric opportunity identified
- Leadership deterioration
- Technical distribution
- Risk/reward no longer favorable
- Constraint or bottleneck resolved

## Rotation Workflow
1. Re-evaluate thesis
2. Compare against current opportunity universe
3. Estimate opportunity cost
4. Determine hold / trim / exit
5. Allocate released capital
6. Record Alpha Checkpoint
7. Publish portfolio actions
