# Volume 0 - Part IV: Scientific Method, Knowledge & Economic Laws

## Scientific Workflow
1. Observe reality
2. Generate competing hypotheses
3. Collect supporting evidence
4. Collect contradicting evidence
5. Construct Knowledge Objects
6. Run historical replay
7. Scientific Council review
8. Red Team challenge
9. Publish validated knowledge
10. Promote to Economic Principle or Economic Law after repeated validation

## Constitutional Rules
- No permanent knowledge without provenance.
- No permanent knowledge without counter-evidence review.
- Every published object is versioned.
- Every law remains open to scientific challenge.
- Learning favors evidence over authority.
