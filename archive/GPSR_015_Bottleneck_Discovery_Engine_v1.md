# GPSR-015 Bottleneck Discovery Engine

Purpose: Identify structural bottlenecks that alter value capture.

## Evaluation Dimensions
- Supply-demand imbalance
- Capacity expansion lead time
- Replacement difficulty
- Switching cost
- Market concentration
- Capital intensity
- Technology uniqueness
- Geopolitical exposure
- Historical analogue similarity

## Processing Pipeline
1. Load Constraint Register
2. Estimate scarcity
3. Forecast persistence
4. Estimate downstream economic impact
5. Compare with Historical World Graphs
6. Classify bottleneck type
7. Publish Bottleneck Register
