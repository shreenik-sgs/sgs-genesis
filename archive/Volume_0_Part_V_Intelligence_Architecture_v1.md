# Volume 0 - Part V: Intelligence Architecture & Cognitive Model

## Three Intelligence Layers
- Scientific Intelligence (Genesis): What enduring economic laws govern reality? -> Economic Laws
- Operational Intelligence (Prometheus): Which laws are active today and where is value migrating? -> Investment Theses
- Executive Intelligence (Personal CIO): What actions should be taken now? -> Portfolio Decisions

## Constitutional Interfaces
- Genesis never makes portfolio decisions.
- Prometheus never creates permanent scientific knowledge.
- Personal CIO never overrides evidence produced by Genesis or Prometheus.
- All communication occurs through canonical graph objects.
- Every recommendation must be traceable to an Economic Law or validated Knowledge Object.
