# GPSR-001 Master Architecture

## Mission
Transform reality into explainable capital allocation decisions.

16. Capital Allocation
17. Continuous Monitoring
18. Exit & Rotation
19. Recursive Learning

## Architecture Rules
- Stocks are outputs, never inputs.
- Persistent World Graphs are long-lived economic structures.
- Themes activate graphs; they do not create them.
- Genesis creates scientific knowledge; Prometheus applies it.
- Every recommendation must be traceable through the discovery pipeline.
- Scientific knowledge is updated only after validation.
