# GPSR-021 Capital Allocation Engine

Purpose: Convert validated opportunities into portfolio allocations.

## Allocation Pipeline
1. Rank opportunities
2. Estimate expected asymmetry
3. Evaluate portfolio interactions
4. Allocate risk budget
5. Recommend position size
6. Generate pyramiding schedule
7. Publish allocation plan

## Allocation Dimensions
- Expected asymmetry
- Historical analogue strength
- Leadership quality
- Technical readiness
- Portfolio correlation
- Liquidity
- Downside risk
- Time horizon
- Capital opportunity cost
