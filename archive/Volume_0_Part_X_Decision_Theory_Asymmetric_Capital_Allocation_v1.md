# Volume 0 - Part X: Decision Theory & Asymmetric Capital Allocation

## Decision Philosophy
- Maximize expected asymmetry, not expected return.
- Decision quality is evaluated separately from outcome quality.
- Capital is continuously compared against the opportunity universe.
- Preserve capital for superior future opportunities.
- Every allocation must trace back to validated Economic Laws.

## Constitutional Allocation Rules
- Never allocate solely from technical signals.
- Position sizing reflects conviction and asymmetry.
- Opportunity cost is evaluated before every allocation.
- Rotate capital only when expected asymmetry materially improves.
- Cash is an intentional portfolio position.
