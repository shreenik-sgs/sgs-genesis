## Normative Requirements
- Every research project MUST begin with a real-world phenomenon.
- Every hypothesis MUST have at least one competing hypothesis.
- Every proposed Economic Law MUST survive historical replay.
- Every publication MUST preserve complete provenance.
- Prometheus MAY operationalize a Knowledge Object before it becomes an Economic Law, but MUST record its provisional status.
